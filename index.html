Open Access Resources

A World of learning, always by your side

e-Magazine

Take your favourite magazine anywhere! Library members can access a range of free digital magazines.You can do this from both library and home computers.

e-Books

We offers free ebooks in every category, from history and law to music, psychology, and science. Users also have access to "bookshelves" for specific content.

e-Journals

Electronic journals have several advantages over traditional printed journals. You can search the contents pages and/or the full text of journals to find articles on a certain subject.

DIGITAL CONTENT

A World of learning, always by your side

Punjab Digital Library

Punjab Digital Library provides full-text access to more than 40,000 e-books, journals, magazines, press clippings.

HEC Database Access

It provides subscription to high quality, peer-reviewed journals, databases, and other electronic content across a wide range of disciplines.

Children Digital Library

Outstanding free Children Resources from around the world designed, in close partnership with children for children.

Audio Library (Bolti Kitabain)

Bolti Kitabain is a web/app based audio listening platform where you can enjoy listening to your favorite books according to your convenience.

Online Dictionaries

A World of learning, always by your side

  • Punjabi

    This Pakistani Punjabi to Urdu dictionary will help you to be bilingual. It would be a great help in the Punjabi to Urdu learning.

  • Urdu

    An online trilingual Urdu dictionary with word meaning, definition, pronunciation, usage, synonym, antonym, idiom, proverb of Urdu words.

  • Sindhi English

    Sindhi to English dictionaries are bilingual dictionaries which provide English equivalents of Sindhi language words.

  • English

    The largest and most trusted free online dictionary for learners of British and American English with definitions, pictures, example sentences, synonyms.
